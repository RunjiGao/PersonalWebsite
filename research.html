Research

My research explores human decision-making, social influence, and digital behavior through experimental, statistical, and computational approaches.

Research Interests

Computational Behavioral Science

Using computational tools and large-scale behavioral data to understand psychological processes and human decision-making.

Judgment and Decision Making

Studying how people evaluate uncertainty, risk, delay, and trade-offs in everyday decisions.

Social and Motivational Psychology

Examining how role models, psychological distance, social information, and advice shape motivation and behavior.

Digital Behavior and Online Information

Investigating how online environments and digital media influence beliefs, attitudes, and public opinion.
